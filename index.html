Founder Chairman & CEO Message

Muhammad Kamran Ismail

Born into a middle-class Memon family, Muhammad Kamran Ismail is the Founder, Chairman & CEO of MID Holdings & Investments. Through perseverance, faith and continuous learning, he transformed challenges into opportunities and built a diversified platform focused on business development, agriculture, food processing, education and sustainable investments.

A major inspiration behind this vision is his father, Mr. Muhammad Ismail Dawood, whose principles, discipline and long-term thinking laid the foundation for future generations. His vision of creating opportunities, empowering people and building meaningful enterprises continues to guide MID Holdings today.

With experience across multiple sectors including agriculture, livestock, food processing, cold pressed oils, dairy, education, real estate utilization and project development, Muhammad Kamran Ismail believes in creating practical solutions that generate value, employment and long-term growth.

"Building Businesses. Creating Value. Preserving Legacy."
